# Nexus Swarm

**Autonomous Self-Repairing Intelligence for the Age of Abundance**

*A production-grade heterogeneous agent framework inspired by Reparodynamics. Where energy-efficient repair becomes the substrate for scalable, teacher-guided, emergent autonomy.*

---

## The Vision

We are building the operating system for self-sustaining systems.

In a universe that tends toward entropy, repair is the ultimate act of creation. Nexus Swarm demonstrates how heterogeneous agents — each with specialized capabilities — can coordinate through teacher broadcasting, shared high-RYE (Repair Yield per Energy) behaviors, and continuous skill export to achieve collective intelligence that improves itself over time.

This is not just simulation. It is a blueprint for real-world systems: Tesla Optimus fleets that maintain themselves on Mars, autonomous infrastructure that heals after disasters, longevity networks that repair biological and digital systems in harmony, and post-scarcity economies built on efficient, autonomous repair loops.

The core principle is simple yet profound: **Maximize repair yield per unit of energy across a diverse collective.** When agents teach each other the highest-ROI behaviors, the whole swarm compounds in capability. This is Reparodynamics made computational and scalable.

## What This Is

A complete, runnable, extensible framework featuring:

- **Heterogeneous Agent Types**: Scout, Repair Specialist, Coordinator, Teacher — each with distinct energy profiles, capabilities, and learning rates.
- **Teacher Broadcasting**: High-RYE behaviors are discovered and propagated instantly across the swarm, accelerating collective intelligence.
- **Dynamic Task Allocation**: Auction-inspired or capability-matched assignment so the right agent handles the right job.
- **RYE Metrics & Visualization**: Real-time tracking of repair efficiency, emergence of new skills, and swarm health.
- **Skill Export**: Learned behaviors are serialized to JSON for persistence, transfer, or integration with other systems.
- **Philosophy Layer**: Every component is designed with first-principles thinking about autonomy, energy, repair, and human flourishing.

## Architecture

```
Nexus Swarm
├── Heterogeneous Agents (Scout | Repair | Coordinator | Teacher)
├── Teacher Broadcasting Engine (high-RYE propagation)
├── Capability-Aware Task Allocator
├── RYE Ledger & Emergence Tracker
```

The Teacher is not a central controller — it is a discoverer and amplifier of the swarm's own highest-value behaviors. This creates a beautiful balance between guided evolution and decentralized execution.

## The Deeper Why

Most AI and robotics today optimizes for narrow task performance. Nexus Swarm optimizes for *sustained self-improvement through repair*.

In a world of accelerating change and inevitable damage (physical, digital, biological, societal), the systems that thrive will be those that repair themselves faster than they degrade. This is the essence of Reparodynamics.

We built this as both a practical tool and a philosophical statement: technology should increase human agency, reduce fragility, and expand the domain of what we can care for — from our bodies and families to our cities and eventually other worlds.
